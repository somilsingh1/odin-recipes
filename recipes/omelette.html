OMELETTE

Description

An omelette is a quick and protein-rich dish made from beaten eggs and cooked in a pan

It is a popular breakfast option that can be customized with vegetables, cheese, or spices.

Simple, tasty, and ready in minutes, an omelette is perfect for a healthy start to your day.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped, optional)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped coriander
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil or butter

STEPS

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them well.

  2. Add onion, green chili, coriander, salt, and pepper. Mix well.

  3. Heat oil or butter in a pan over medium heat.

  4. Pour the egg mixture into the pan and spread evenly.

  5. Cook for 2â€“3 minutes until set, then flip and cook the other side.

  6. Fold and serve hot.
