PASTA

description

Pasta is a delicious and versatile Italian dish made with boiled pasta and a flavorful sauce

It can be prepared in many styles, such as creamy white sauce or tangy tomato sauce, and is perfect for lunch or dinner.

This simple pasta recipe is quick to make and loaded with vegetables and rich flavors

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups pasta
  • (finely chopped)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (capsicum, corn, carrots)
  • 2–3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • 2 tablespoons oil

STEPS

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until soft, then drain.

  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic and onions.

  3. Add vegetables and cook for 2–3 minutes.

  4. Add tomato sauce, salt, chili flakes, and oregano.

  5. Mix in the boiled pasta and cook for 2 minutes.

  6. Garnish with cheese and serve hot.
